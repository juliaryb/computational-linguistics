# Task Definitions

These are general descriptions (often oversimplified), the final prompts (used in evaluation) are defined in `prompts/`. During the **prompt engineering** phase one or two alternatice prompts were tested (on dev examples) with more detail than the ones listed here and the one that provided the "nicest" output was added to the .json files in `prompts/` and were later applied to the validation examples.

## 1. Instruction Following (IFEval-style)
Description: Follow explicit instructions and constraints precisely.

## 2. Logical Reasoning
Description: Solve a short logical reasoning problem.

## 3. Creative Writing
Description: Produce a short creative text under given constraints.

## 4. Code Generation
Description: Generate correct and readable code for a given task.

## 5. Reading Comprehension
Description: Answer questions based on a provided passage.

## 6. Common Sense Reasoning
Description: Answer a question requiring everyday reasoning.

## 7. Language Understanding & Ambiguity
Description: Resolve ambiguity in language and interpret meaning correctly.

## 8. Factual Knowledge & Retrieval
Description: Answer a factual knowledge question.

## 9. Mathematical Problem Solving
Description: Solve a short mathematical problem.

## 10. Ethical Reasoning & Nuance
Description: Analyze a situation with ethical considerations.
